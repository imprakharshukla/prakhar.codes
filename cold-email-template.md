# Cold Email Template

## Subject Line
Founding engineer who built $140K product

## Email Body

```
Hi {name},

{specific observation about company/product} - this is exactly the type of problem I love solving.

I built Andronix to 2.5M+ downloads and $140K+ revenue, then joined Yobr (Norway) as founding engineer. Also built Lumoflo, a multi-tenant e-commerce platform handling inventory, payments, and shipping at scale.

Recently been working with {relevant tech stack that matches their company}.

Looking for my next early-stage opportunity. Would love to chat about {company_name}.

Here's my work: prakhar.codes

Best,
Prakhar
```

## Variables to Fill
- `{name}` - Recipient's first name
- `{specific observation}` - One sentence about what they're building that interests you
- `{relevant tech stack}` - 2-3 technologies from their stack that you have experience with
- `{company_name}` - Their company name
